About Us

At Red Robin, we’re all about serving delicious, handcrafted burgers made from the freshest ingredients. Our passion is creating craveable food with bold flavors and memorable experiences — whether you're dining in, ordering online, or grabbing something to-go. Come for the food, stay for the flavor!

Red Robin is a place where families and friends gather. We're proud to be a well-loved staple in communities across the country.
